Campus

Spread over a vibrant eight-acre campus at Lohegaon Pune, KIS is a proposed ICSE curriculum school. We provide the latest methods and infrastructure in the sector of education and teaching methodology. Our curriculum is founded on the Pancha Koshas, the five pillars of well-being, uniting the physical, emotional, intellectual, creative and spiritual aspects that are essential to the complete blossoming of every child. Kaveri International School is set in the history-rich environment of Pune.
Our beautiful and sprawling 8.5-acre campus has a state of the art learning environment designed to inspire students to discover their strengths and to nurture them. We provide up to date facilities like laboratories, Science centre, art and music studios, libraries, gymnasium, spacious playing and athletic fields and separate fenced playgrounds for pre-school children. The campus will include separate areas for pre-primary, primary, secondary and high schools.
